I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Orléans, working on computational imaging and machine learning methods for hyperspectral image super-resolution and Earth observation.

I completed my PhD on AI for computer-aided diagnosis of mycetoma, focusing on segmentation and classification of histopathological microscopic images and computational approaches for neglected tropical diseases. My research has since expanded toward remote sensing, computational imaging, and resource-efficient AI methods.

Alongside research, I have more than ten years of experience in higher education teaching, mentoring, and supervision in mathematics and quantitative disciplines, with interests spanning applied mathematics, AI, and scientific computing.

Feel free to contact me about collaborations, teaching opportunities, or interdisciplinary research.

## Interests

- Computational imaging 
- Medical image analysis
- Remote sensing & hyperspectral imaging
- Image super-resolution
- Resource-efficient & self-supervised learning
- Applied mathematics

## Research Overview

My research lies at the intersection of:

- Artificial Intelligence
- Computer Vision
- Computational Imaging
- Medical Imaging
- Remote Sensing
- Machine Learning

I develop computational methods for image analysis, reconstruction, and enhancement, with applications spanning healthcare and Earth observation.

## Current Research Topics

### Computational Imaging & Super-Resolution
Image reconstruction, super-resolution, and enhancement methods for scientific imaging.

### Medical Imaging
AI methods for segmentation, analysis, and diagnostic support.

### Remote Sensing & Hyperspectral Imaging
Earth observation, hyperspectral image analysis, and environmental monitoring.

### Trustworthy AI
Robust and reliable machine learning methods for scientific applications.

## Future Interests

- Foundation models for scientific imaging
- Interdisciplinary AI
- Resource-efficient deep learning
- AI for global challenges
